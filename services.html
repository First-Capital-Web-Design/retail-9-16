General Contracting Services
First Capital Builders is positioned to be the General Contractor for your next real estate project. Our experienced professionals will work with you from project inception to effectively bid, construct, and close out each project to maximize project profitability. We have significant experience in all phases of construction including infrastructure, commercial, residential, and tenant improvements.
Project and Construction Management
Our project managers offer expertise during all phases of your project to effectively and creatively achieve your objectives.

First Capital Builders provides comprehensive project/construction management services to owners and users on a local, regional and national basis. First Capital Builders is committed to providing project management services of the highest professional standards to owners of, and investors in, commercial, residential and investment properties.

We are experienced construction professionals, who operate within a formal control system, offer assistance in budget preparations, design review, construction techniques, regulatory codes and other areas.

Project/Construction Management services include pre-construction services, not limited to design professionals, validating constructionability, budget estimates, value engineering, development of schedules, contract bid analysis, payment management, including review and approval, owner representation, addressing contract administration, craft and tenant coordination, and conflict resolution.
