Family of Companies

What We Do

Over the past five years, the company has specialized in many different asset types ranging from land entitlements, residential, commercial including light industrial, self storage, service stations with a QSR, (Quick Service Restaurant), and C Store (Convenience Store) component, multiple residential sub division development projects and has added multiple hospitality assets and retail projects to its asset mix. We also procured principal equity positions within a direct residential mortgage lending platform with full integration ranging from origination through securitization of mortgage backed securities as well as its ownership in a thriving brokerage partnership primarily driving commercial real estate finance opportunities as well as serving in the capacity of new deal origination. The company today primarily focuses in 4 capacities, development and construction, lending, brokerage/advisory and long-term assets making for a diversified platform.

COMPREHENSIVE PROJECT/CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT SERVICES

At First Capital Builders, we take a comprehensive approach to all of your construction needs. From initial design meetings, value engineering advice, cost savings through construction, and ensuring a smooth close out, First Capital Builders successfully completes each project on time and on budget. The experienced professionals at First Capital Builders have consistently been able to add value, mitigate risk, and enhance profitability for their clients.

OWNS & OPERATES MULTIPLE RETAIL LOCATIONS

First Capital Retail, LLC (FCR) was established as the acquisition, development, and ownership entity of all franchise and non-franchise retail activities with the First Capital companies. Currently, FCR owns and operates multiple retail locations across the State of California including Circle K Convenience Stores, Cinnabon Bakeries, and is currently underwriting the acquisition a large multi-unit Cinnabon portfolio along with a Wendy’s portfolio. FCR’s main acquisition focus remains on nationally recognized brands, positive cash flow enterprises, and high visibility locations.

OPERATIONS ADMINISTRATION

First Capital Management, LLC (FCM) provides operations administration to the various First Capital companies. FCM is responsible for bookkeeping, human resources, payroll, policies, employee support, and federal/state/industry compliance management in a variety of businesses that includes hospitality, retail, financial, and construction. In addition, FCM facilitates the development of smooth on boarding transition plans. The goal is to provide excellent administration of business practices to create the highest level of efficiency possible within each individual business and the organization as a whole.
